# Business Performance KPIs

## Objective
Finding key business drivers to support data-driven decision making

## Core KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Unique Customers
- Average Order Value

## Performance Views
- Revenue trends over time
- Country-level revenue contribution
- Category- level revenue contribution

## Business Questions Answered
1. How is overall business performance trending?
2. Which countries are driving or lagging revenue?
3. Which product categories contribute most to revenue?
4. How efficient is the business in generating revenue per order?

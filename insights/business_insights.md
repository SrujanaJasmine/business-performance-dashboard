## Business Performance Insights

## Conclusions

1. The business demonstrates strong overall performance, generating approximately 700,000 in total revenue from around 1,000 orders, with an average order value of 690.

2. Revenue trends show clear seasonality, with January, May, July, and September recording the highest revenue levels.

3. South America is the strongest revenue-generating region, while Asia records the lowest sales performance.

4. Books and Electronics contribute the largest share of total revenue, whereas Clothing generates the lowest revenue. However, sold quantities are relatively balanced across all categories, indicating that revenue differences are primarily price-driven.

5. On average, each customer contributes up to 3,200 in revenue, reflecting strong customer value.

## Key Recommendations

- Prioritize investment and retention strategies in top-performing countries while testing targeted growth initiatives in lower-performing regions.

- Align marketing campaigns and inventory planning with observed seasonal revenue patterns.

- Focus merchandising and supply planning on high-revenue categories to maximize overall performance.
